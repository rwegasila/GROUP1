About Me

My name is Lisa Njau. I was born in Moshi, I am a second-year student at Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST), where I'm diving deep into the world of Data Science. I've always been fascinated by the power of data and how it can be used to solve real-world problems. In my studies, I've been learning all about programming languages like Python, C, and C++, and I've been having a blast building websites using cool tools like Django, HTML5, and CSS3.

One of my favorite things to do is to explore data and uncover interesting patterns and insights. I've gotten pretty good at crunching numbers and visualizing data in graphs and charts so that everyone can understand what's going on. I've also been learning how to build computer models that can predict things based on data, which is super exciting.

Outside of my classes, I've been working on some cool projects. Right now, I'm in the process of creating a website for an agriculture company called Agrifarm. It's been a challenging but rewarding experience, and I can't wait to see the final product. I've also had the opportunity to work on other projects, like designing websites for companies such as Moze Agriculture and Duka Ulipo, as well as creating eye-catching business cards that people love.

I'm a big fan of being creative, and I love using software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, and Figma to bring my ideas to life. Whether it's designing a new website or coming up with a unique business card design, I'm always up for a challenge. I'm constantly learning and growing, and I'm excited to see where my journey in Data Science takes me next.
